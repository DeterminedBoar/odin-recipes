Chocolate Milk Recipe

Description

A recipe for chocolate milk

Ingredients

  • 1 container of chocolate milk mix
  • 1 jug of milk
  • 1 bag of sugar

Steps

  1. Scoop a couple of spoons of chocolate milk mix into a empty cup
  2. Scoop 1 spoonful of sugar into the same cup
  3. Pour milk into cup
  4. Mix until both powders are dissolved and enjoy!
