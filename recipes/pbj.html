Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe

Description

A basic recipe for a peanut butter and jelly sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle of jelly
  • 1 bottle of peanut butte
  • 2 slices of honey oat bread

Steps

  1. Scoop jelly with knife and spread it on 1 slice bread until bread is covered
  2. Scoop peanut butter with knife and spread it on the other slice of bread until bread is covered
  3. Sandwich breads together with the condiments touching
  4. Enjoy
