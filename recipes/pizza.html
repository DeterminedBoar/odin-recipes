Pizza Recipe

Description

A simple easy recipe for cheese pizza! Can be made in just an hour!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of pre-made pizza dough
  • 2 bags of shredded mozzarella
  • 1-2 bottles of prego or ragu sauce

Steps

  1. Flatten out pizza dough into a disk shape
  2. Pour a generous amount of sauce onto the dough
  3. Sprinkle the cheese on the pizza
  4. Place pizza on a metal baking pan
  5. Preheat oven to 360 degrees
  6. Once oven is done preheating, place the pizza into the oven on the bottom rack
  7. Wait 10 minutes and then pull out pizza!
